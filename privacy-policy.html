Privacy Policy for Breezer

At Breezer ("we", "our", or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Breezer mobile application (the "App") and associated services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

Breezer is an app designed to help users track their snus consumption, set personalized limits, and analyze their consumption habits over time. We understand the importance of your privacy, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information. We want to ensure complete transparency about what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of data to provide and improve our Service:

2.1 Personal Information

When you register and use the App, we collect the following personal data:

  • Email, username, and password: Used for account creation and authentication.
  • Profile picture (optional): You may choose to upload a profile picture for personalization within the app.
2.2 Consumption Data

We collect data related to your snus consumption habits, including:

  • Snus type, pack size, price per pack, and consumption limits: You can track your consumption by entering the type of snus, its pack size, and price.
  • Consumption events: Records each time you log your consumption.
  • Weekly, monthly, and yearly consumption summaries: These provide you with insights into your snus usage, spending, and habits.

This information is processed only to deliver the app's functionality, and is used solely for tracking and analytical purposes.

2.3 Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain technical data when you use the app, including:

  • Device information: Such as device type, model, and manufacturer.
  • Usage patterns: How often you use the app and which features you interact with.
  • IP Address: For maintaining the security of our app.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication: Your email, username, and password help create and secure your account.
  • Personalization: The profile picture helps personalize your experience.
  • Statistics and insights: Consumption data is used to provide insights into your habits and to help you set goals and limits for your consumption.

We do not use your data for personalized marketing, ads, or sharing with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Sharing of Data with Third Parties

We may share your data with the following third parties to help provide and improve our Service:

  • Appwrite Cloud (for data storage and management): We use Appwrite Cloud to store and manage user data. Appwrite ensures secure data handling practices and complies with relevant data protection standards.
  • RevenueCat (for cash reporting and payment integrations): RevenueCat handles payments and cash reporting, ensuring secure financial data processing.

We ensure that all third parties we work with take appropriate security measures to protect your data.

5. User Rights and Data Control

You have the following rights and control over your personal data:

  • Profile management: You can update your profile picture and consumption data directly through the app.
  • Data deletion: If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so by sending an email to breezer-app@gmail.com or by going to Settings -> Account Settings -> Delete Account in the app.
  • Data access: You can request a summary of the data we hold about you. Please contact us at breezer-app@gmail.com to submit such a request.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. To protect your personal information, we use the following security practices:

  • Encryption: Your data is stored securely using encryption provided by Appwrite Cloud.
  • Access control: Only authorized personnel have access to your data.
  • No transfer outside your region: Your data is not transferred to other countries and is stored securely within the region Appwrite Cloud operates in.

While we take all reasonable precautions to protect your data, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we strive to implement robust security measures.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Breezer app does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies beyond those required to track your consumption data. We do not track your activity for marketing or advertising purposes.

8. Policy Changes and Notification

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. If we make significant changes to how we handle your data, we will notify you through the app, by email, or through app store updates. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

9. Your Rights Under Data Protection Laws

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You have the right to access the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request that we delete your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to restriction: You may ask us to limit the use of your data.
  • Right to portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: If you provided consent for the processing of your data, you can withdraw it at any time.

For more details or to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at breezer-app@gmail.com.

10. Marketing and Promotional Communications

At present, Breezer does not engage in sending promotional communications, but this may change in the future. If we do send you marketing emails, you will have the option to opt-out by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email or updating your preferences in the app settings.

11. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete all associated data as per our retention policy.

12. Children's Privacy

Breezer is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. If you are under 17, please do not use the app. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 17. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from someone under 17, we will take steps to delete that data.
